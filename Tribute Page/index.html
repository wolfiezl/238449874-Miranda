Liam Payne: Singer, Songwriter, and Performer.

About Liam Payne

James Payne was an English singer and songwriter. He rose to fame as a member of the boy band One Direction. Payne made his debut as a singer in 2008 when he auditioned for the British television series The X Factor. After being eliminated in the competition, he auditioned again in 2010 and was put into a group with four other contestants to form One Direction, which went on to become one of the best-selling boy bands of all time. Payne worked with other producers under the monikers "Big Payno" and "Payno" creating remixes for songs by his group and Cheryl. After One Direction's hiatus, Payne signed a recording deal with Republic Records in North America. In May 2017, Payne released "Strip That Down" as the lead single from his debut album. It peaked at number three on the UK Singles Chart and number ten on the US Billboard Hot 100, being certified platinum in both countries. His debut album, LP1, was released in December 2019. He has sold over 18 million singles in under three years since One Direction's hiatus, and over 3.9 billion career streams in that span.

Timeline of Liam Payne

  • 1993: Birth of Liam Payne

    Liam James Payne was born on August 29, 1993, in Wolverhampton, England.

  • 2008: First Audition for The X Factor

    Liam auditioned for The X Factor UK at the age of 14 but didn't make it past the initial stages.

  • 2010: Formation of One Direction

    Liam joined One Direction in 2010, forming the globally successful boy band.

  • 2012: One Direction's First World Tour

    One Direction embarked on their first world tour, the "Up All Night Tour," which was a major success worldwide.

  • 2013: One Direction's Breakthrough Year

    One Direction's album "Midnight Memories" reached the top of the charts, marking a significant milestone in the band's career.

  • 2016: Solo Career Debut

    Liam Payne started his solo career, releasing his debut single "Strip That Down" in 2017.

  • 2017: Birth of His Son

    Liam became a father to a son named Bear Payne with his ex-girlfriend Cheryl Cole in March 2017.

  • 2018: Collaboration with Rita Ora

    Liam Payne collaborated with Rita Ora for the hit single "For You," which was featured on the soundtrack for the movie Fifty Shades Freed.

  • 2019: Launch of "LP1" Album

    Liam released his debut solo album "LP1," featuring hits like "Stack It Up" and "Polaroid."

  • 2021: New Music on the Way

    Liam teased new music and hinted at the release of a new album in 2021.

  • 2022: Social Media Growth and Advocacy

    Liam used his platform to raise awareness on social issues, including mental health advocacy and climate change activism.

  • 2024: Release of New Single "Teardrops"

    Liam Payne released his new single "Teardrops" on February 14, 2024, which quickly became a hit, showcasing his continued evolution as a solo artist.

  • 2024: Tragic Passing

    Tragically, Liam Payne passed away on October 16, 2024, after falling from his hotel balcony in Buenos Aires, Argentina. His death shocked the world, and fans everywhere mourn the loss of a talented artist and beloved figure.
